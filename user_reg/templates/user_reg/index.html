services

Why use My Event Planner? Take a look at what we have to offer, both to people planning events, and businesses offering events-related services.

Event Planning on the Go

Plan up to three events at a time! My Event Planner helps you manage your guet list, tasks, and budget for each event. A great event starts with the planning. We'll make sure yours is one to remember.

Contact Service Providers

You can find hundreds of service providers on My Event Planner. What's better, is there's no need to take down their phone number or memorize some email addresses. Simply send them a message directly fom our platform!

Ratings & Reviews

It can be difficult knowing which service providers to choose. On My Event Planner, you can browse each service provider's ratings and reviews, as well as rate and review them yourself!

For Service Providers

On My Event Planner, we've got the service providers covered as well. If you offer any services related to events, sign up on our platform! You can reach more customers and make announcements on the platform, as well as communicate with clients directly.
